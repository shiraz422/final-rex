About Us

Welcome to Rex Motors

Your Trusted Source for Vehicle History Insights

At Rex Motors, we are dedicated to empowering vehicle owners and buyers with comprehensive and accurate vehicle history reports across the UK and Ireland. Our mission is to provide both individual and commercial customers with the essential insights needed to make informed decisions when buying, selling, or maintaining vehicles.

Founded on the principles of transparency and reliability, we understand the critical role that trustworthy vehicle data plays in today’s marketplace. Our detailed reports cover a wide range of vital information, including accident history, ownership changes, market valuations, mileage verification, and any open recalls. With this knowledge, our customers can confidently navigate their vehicle transactions, ensuring their investments are secure.

We pride ourselves on the quality of our data, sourced from reputable government agencies, salvage auctions, and proprietary databases. Our user-friendly platform allows you to obtain a detailed PDF report quickly and efficiently, putting the information you need at your fingertips.

At Rex Motors, our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering. We believe in providing exceptional service and support, helping you every step of the way. Thank you for choosing Rex Motors as your trusted partner in vehicle history reporting. Together, we can bridge the information gap and empower you to make sound vehicle decisions.
